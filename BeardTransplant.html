What is Beard Transplant?

Beard transplant is the transport of the hair follicles taken from different parts of the body to the beard area. In classical terms, this procedure is no different from hair transplantation. This procedure has been developed for people who are beardless or have no beard because of an accident or similar reasons or who have a little beard. After the beard transplant, people have a natural beard. Thus, they have the appearance of their dreams.

Who is Eligible for Beard Transplant?

Beard Transplant is a procedure that can be applied to almost anyone who does not have a serious health problem. The most suitable candidates are:
  • People with thin beard or who are beardless
  • Trans people who want to have an appearance with a beard
  • People who want to hide scars with beard.
  • People who want to have a more masculine look

Beard Transplant Consultation

Before the beard transplant, you should make a consultation with your doctor to determine the details. In this consultation, your doctor should get information about your medical history. The required information is:
  • Past surgeries
  • Present health condition
  • Regularly used drugs
  • Allergies
  • Smoking
  • Alcohol use
After this consultation, your eligibility for beard transplant is determined. Again at this stage, you can convey all your expectations to your doctor.

Beard Plant Risks and Complications

Although beard transplant is a simple procedure, there may be some risks and complications if attention is not paid. Therefore, people who want to have the beard transplant should prefer doctors and clinics specialized in their fields. Main risks are:
  • Infection
  • Light Bleeding
  • Mild Pain
  • Swelling
  • Redness

Preparation for Beard Transplant Surgery

Things to be done before the beard transplant surgery are similar to that of hair transplantation. These are:
  • Alcohol and smoking should be abandoned a week earlier
  • Consumption of drinks like coffee containing caffeine should be abandoned 3 days earlier
  • Blood thinners such as aspirin should be stopped a week earlier.
  • On the surgery day, comfy clothes that won't touch your scalp during getting dressed or undressed should be preferred such as tops with zipper or clothes with a wide collar

Things to Consider after Beard Transplant

  • Try to keep your face dry for the first five days after the beard transplant.
  • Shave only with scissors for the first 6 months. If shavers are used before 6 months, they can damage the beard follicles.
  • Try to protect your head and facial area from impacts for the first 2 weeks.
  • Try to follow your doctors all instructions without exception.

Beard Transplant Frequently Asked Questions

Is the beard transplant safe?
As the person's hair follicles are used, the beard transplant is a completely natural procedure. Therefore, it is certainly a safe procedure.
What is the earliest age for the beard transplant?
As there is the possibility of beard growth until the age of 22, the beard transplant is not recommended until this age.
Will there be scars left on my face?
Absolutely no! Beard transplant is a natural surgical operation. There are no scars left and no one will understand you have a beard transplant
When can I have a bath?
: You must keep your face dry for the first 5 days. You can have a bath the following day without water touching your face.
When can I go back to work?
: If you do not work in a heavy work that causes you to sweat, you can start the next day.
