YOUR JOURNEY

Before you come to Turkey
An online interview will be held regarding the cosmetic procedure you need. In this meeting, your eligibility for the operation will be evaluated. If you have an obstacle to the operation, our specialist surgeons set everything up for you and they will wait for your arrival to Turkey.
Otel Reservation
It will be more than enough if you just book a flight that matches your timeline and let us know the details. We will organize all the entire process related to preparations for the operation day, pick-up at the airport with VIP vehicles and your hotel accommodation. Just come to the airport and leave the rest to us.
Arrival in Turkey
Our VIP transfer team will meet you at the airport and provide your transfer to the hotel. He/she will then contact the clinic for the start of your treatment. All transfers between hotel, clinic and airport will be made by this professional team.
