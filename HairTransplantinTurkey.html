HAIR TRANSPLANTATION TURKEY

All patients who have searched for hair transplantation surely reach lots of information related to hair transplantation in Turkey. There is a great number of patients who choose to undergo hair transplantation in Turkey due to the various benefits. Factors such as the expertise of Turkey in hair transplantation and quality of health services has been very effective in this issue. Well, are the advantages only limited to the ones mentioned above? Of course not! Come along, let's examine other advantages in Turkey.

Why Choose Turkey?

Turkey is one of the world's top 10 countries in terms of health tourism. Many people have plastic surgery operations, especially hair transplantation, in this country. In Turkey, it is possible to find the same high-tech health care just as in European countries. So, how come the same quality of health services in Turkey cost 50% - 70% cheaper?
  • Medical costs in Turkey are cheaper than the ones in other countries
  • Due to the difference in the exchange rate, currencies like Pound and Euro are much more valuable than TL.
  • Lower labor costs
  • Costs become lower thanks to the government supporting health tourism
Apart from these advantages in terms of cost, Turkey has another advantage in terms of location as it is near the European countries. After a 3-4 hour flight from London to Turkey, you can have your hair transplantation operation in this country. In this way, you can enjoy your new hair without spending too much time and waiting in line like in the UK. Every year tens of thousands of patients are traveling to Turkey for hair transplantation surgery. Due to the fact that the same procedures and the same quality of health care are more affordable, Turkey emerges as the most popular destination in the transplantation.

Comparison of Costs

Let's look at a simple cost calculation in the UK and Turkey for hair transplantation. Medical costs and workmanship in the UK are relatively high compared to Turkey and this difference is reflected in the costs. For example;
  • • The average cost of hair transplantation in Turkey is £2.500
  • • In the UK, the average cost for hair transplantation is around £ 9,000.
These are the main reasons for the low cost of the hair transplantation in Turkey despite the qualities of the surgery is in the same quality or even better. These average prices may vary depending on the number of grafts and other medical needs. However, there is a huge difference of about 70% in general.
