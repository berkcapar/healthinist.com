What is FUE?

The FUE technique is to collect the grafts from the donor area one by one and then transfer them to the related area in the same way. The most important difference of this process compared to the methods used in the past is that it is scarless. There was scars in the donor are after the old methods and these negatively effect the general outlook of people. With the FUE technique, this negativity is completely eliminated. In the FUE technique, which is a very comfortable method, an average of 5,000 grafts can be transplanted in a single session. This means that hair transplantation usually ends after a single session. Since the hair is picked one by one, there is no scar and the grafts are protected. This means an increase in the success rate.

Who Is Eligible For FUE?

FUE hair transplantation is a method that can be applied to almost everyone who doesn’t have a very important health problem. The most suitable candidates for the FUE method are:
  • Men or women with mostly or partly shedding hair
  • This with hairline going backwards
  • Those who have partial baldness due to Androgenic Alopecia
  • Those who want to have beard and mustache transplantation
  • Kaş ekimi yaptırmak isteyenler

Features and Advantages of FUE Hair Transplantation

FUE is still one of the most preferred methods of hair transplantation for many years. The reasons why it is so preferred are:
  • Transplantation is carried out at the same angle in accordance with the angle of the existing hair. Thus, a completely natural image is provided.
  • There is a guarantee of over 90% success.
  • The transplanted hair is not lost for a lifetime.
  • It is applied as a painless procedure thanks to local anesthesia.
  • There is no scar.
  • Other people cannot understand that hair is transplanted.

FUE Consultation

Before defining the day for FUE operation, you need to have a consultation with your doctor. At this meeting, your doctor will ask a number of questions about your medical history and current health status. They are:
  • The surgeries you have had so far
  • Medicines you use regularly
  • Current health status
  • Allergic condition
  • Smoking and alcohol use
This information to be obtained by your doctor will determine whether you are eligible for the operation. Feel free to share your expectations and questions regarding the FUE operation with your doctor.

How is FUE Hair Transplantation Carried Out?

Some steps must be followed in this process. These steps and their explanations are given below.
1
Anestezi
Local anesthesia is applied to the donor area where the grafts will be collected and the area to be transplanted. Thus, the patient is completely prevented from suffering during the procedure.
2
Collecting the Grafts
One of the most important steps in the FUE technique is this stage. Grafts are collected individually. In this process, no incisions and stitches are required. Thus, a negative situation such as a scar in the donor region is never experienced. After the grafts are collected, they are left in a special solution. Thus, it is protected from external influences and prepared for transplantation during period.
3
Opening the Channels
Greftler toplandıktan sonra ya da toplanma aşamasında iken kanallar açılır. Kanallar, greftlere uygun olmalıdır. Ayrıca bu kanallar belirli bir açı ile açılır. Açı sayesinde ekilen saçlar, doğal saç stiline uygun olur. Böylece ekilen saçlar dışardan kesinlikle anlaşılmaz, hiçbir şekilde belli olmaz.
4
Transplanting the Grafts
At the last stage, grafts are placed in the opened channels. Thus, the process is completed.

FUE Hair Transplant Risks and Complications

Although FUE hair transplantation is known as an operation, its risks are quite a few - almost none. These risks are kept to a minimum by a team of specialists and experienced staff.
  • Mild pain
  • Swelling
  • Temporary redness
  • Light bleeding
  • Infection

Getting Ready for FUE Operation

There are some points you should be aware of at least 1 week before your FUE hair transplantation surgery. Among them are:
  • Smoking and alcohol use should be stopped for at least 4 days. This is very important to increase the success of the operation and prevent possible risks.
  • Stop consuming caffeine-containing foods and drinks such as coffee 3 days in advance.
  • Do not use any hair care products such as hair gel and spray in this process.
  • Stop using aspirin and blood-thinning medications at least 7 days in advance.
  • Choose a wide collar or zippered clothes in which you can be comfortable on the day of operation.
  • Do not forget to follow the other instructions that your doctor will recommend.

Care After FUE

After the FUE hair transplantation surgery, the care phase begins. You can think of this as a complementary treatment. To get the best results from hair transplantation, you should also pay attention to the following:
  • Bandage removal and first washing is done in the clinic by your doctor.
  • Subsequent washes are applied by you as recommended by your doctor. It is recommended to wash your hair every day for 15 days.
  • Be careful not to press the towel and rub while drying your hair. Dry with gentle touches without damaging the area.
  • You should not use a hairdryer. If you have to, you can use it provided that it is as long as an arm length without turning the hot mode on.
  • You should not swim in the sea or pool for 3-4 weeks.
  • You should protect the area from the sun for 3-4 weeks.
  • Protect the area from blows for 2 weeks.
  • Try to sleep in a relatively upright position in the first days to prevent temporary bloating.

FUE Frequently Asked Questions

Is the FUE method safe?
It is absolutely safe and minimum risk operation if performed by a specialist surgeon and experienced staff.
Do I need to shave before I come to the operation?
No. We do this for you at the clinic.
When can I get back to work?
Since FUE hair transplantation is a simple operation, if you do not have a heavy job, you can return to work even on the same day. However, if you have time, it is recommended to rest on the first day.
What to do if there are not enough grafts?
The number of grafts is generally sufficient, but in cases where it is not available, it can also be reinforced from body hair.
When can I continue my exercises?
After 1 week, you can go back to light exercises and after 2 weeks, you can return to your tighter exercises.
Q: Do I need a dressing after the operation?
Yes. On the day after hair transplantation, your bandage is removed and the first washing process is applied by your doctor.
