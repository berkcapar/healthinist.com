Eyebrow Transplantation

Eyebrows are one of the important parts that affect the facial appearance. Therefore, eyebrows that have fallen over time because of different reasons cause a change in appearance. This change is generally negative. To eliminate this negativity, the procedure known as eyebrow transplantation has been developed. Occurrence of eyebrow falling is considered to be a beauty defect. This is not specific to women; eyebrow loss can also be seen in men. So, it should be known that eyebrow transplantation is performed regardless of gender. You can get rid of the bald points in your eyebrow thanks to this procedure.

What is Eyebrow Transplantation?

Eyebrow transplantation can be expressed as the transfer of grafts taken from different parts of the body to the eyebrow area. It is very similar to hair transplantation known to almost everyone. However, it should be noted that there are some differences. Especially, the fact that eyebrows are entirely seen from outside makes is mandatory to be very careful during the procedure. Different techniques can be applied for eyebrow transplantation. However, it is not wrong to tell that FUE and DIE techniques are the most common ones today. In this procedure first, the neck area is examined for the grafts. If there aren't sufficient grafts here, they are collected from different parts of the body. But, it can be said that the neck area is more than enough for the grafts. The grafts taken from here one by one are then planted in the eyebrow area with special techniques. In this way, the bald areas in the eyebrows are eliminated. Reshaping of the eyebrows is also possible with this procedure.

Who is Eligible for Eyebrow Transplantation?

Eyebrow transplantation is a procedure that can be applied to everyone healthy. This procedure is generally a solution for the below cases:
  • People with very thin eyebrows
  • People who want to have thicker eyebrows
  • People who have bald areas in their eyebrows

Eyebrow Transplantation Consultation

Although eyebrow transplantation is considered to be a simple surgery, it shouldn’t be forgotten that it is a surgical procedure. Before this procedure, you should make a consultation with your doctor. In this consultation, your doctor should get information about your medical history. The required information is:
  • Present health condition
  • Allergies
  • Smoking and Alcohol use
  • Past surgeries
  • Regularly used drugs
Here, it is very important to share the true information with your doctor. In this way, possible risks and complications can be prevented more effectively. At the consultation stage, you can also ask questions to your doctor. You can relax by asking how your eyebrows will look like after the transplantation or other questions you have in mind. The replies you get here will help to eliminate the negative thoughts in your mind.

How is Eyebrow Transplantation Performed?

There are some stages to be followed in the eyebrow transplantation. These stages and related information are as follows:
1
Preparation of the Area to be planted
This is the stage in which the area to be planted is determined. The area you have decided with your doctor is marked. In this way, the eyebrow plantation is done in the planned area.
2
Anesthesia
Local anesthesia is applied to your eyebrows. Therefore, there is no possibility of feeling pain.
3
Collection of Grafts
This is the stage in which the grafts to be planted in your eyebrows are collected. Grafts are generally collected one by one from the neck area. Different parts are rarely preferred. The collected grafts may be put in a special solution depending on the technique to be applied.
4
Planting of the Grafts
The collected grafts are planted at a certain angle. Special pens or pins are used.
5
General Control
After the eyebrow transplantation, a general check is conducted. During this check, detailed information about the things to be careful at are explained to you by your doctor.

Eyebrow Transplantation Risks and Possible Complications

Risks and possible complications of eyebrow transplantation are quite low. Moreover, the possibility of these risks and complications to be permanent is very unlikely. These side effects and complications are as follows:
  • Bruising
  • Swelling
  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Infection
As mentioned above, these are rare side effects.

Preparation for Eyebrow Transplantation Surgery

Preparations you will make before eyebrow transplantation will directly affect the success of the procedure. These are as follows:
  • Follow your doctor’s instructions about the medication you will continue taking and the ones to stop.
  • Stop smoking a few days before the surgery. Similarly, don’t smoke during the initial periods after the surgery.
  • Share with your doctor all the details about your health.
  • Come to the clinic wearing comfy clothes that won’t touch your head when getting dressed or undressed like shirts.

Things to Consider after Eyebrow Transplantation

  • Don’t consume alcohol in the first month.
  • Don’t have a sexual intercourse in the first week after the surgery.
  • Don’t smoke in the first month after the surgery.
  • Stay away from the exercises like swimming or sauna that will sweat and tire you for a while.
  • Follow the other instructions given by your doctor.

Eyebrow Transplantation Frequently Asked Questions

Is eyebrow transplantation safe?
Yes, it is. The fact that the techniques developed for hair transplantation are applied here is the main reason for its safety. Apart from that, only the grafts taken from the person him/herself are transplanted. Therefore, conditions like histocompatibility or different allergy conditions are not possible.
Can the transplanted eyebrows be dyed?
There is no problem in dyeing them after the recovery period is completed. There won’t be any difference between the transplanted eyebrows and your natural eyebrows. Therefore, you can apply all the procedures you have for your natural eyebrows to the transplanted eyebrows.
Will the growth rates of the eyebrows be different?
The grafts necessary for eyebrow transplantation are taken from the neck area. Therefore, their growth rate before the recovery period may be different. But after a while, this difference in growth rate will disappear.
Will there be scars after the surgery?
No, there won’t. It is not possible to have any scars both in the area where the grafts are taken and the area where the eyebrow transplantation is performed.
Will the shape of the eyebrow be as I want?
Yes, it will. The shape of the eyebrow is determined by you and your doctor before the surgery.
When do I recover?
Recovery period for eyebrow transplantation is very short. If you do not work in heavy work, you can return your work the next day. But, if the appearance of crusting in your eyebrow area bothers you, it may be necessary to have a week off. The recovery period is completed in one week and you can go back to your normal life.
