Patient Guide

When you decide for the hair transplant surgery, you will be obsessed by many questions such as “When will the procedure be done?, How many days will it take?, How should I do my planning?”. We have prepared this patient guide for you so that you can have more transparent information and make your planning much more comfortably.
1
Hair Analysis
On the first day you come to our clinic, the type of your hair loss is determined. Then, your hair in the donor area is analyzed. This analysis is important in order to find out whether the number of grafts and the roots of the grafts are suitable for transplanting. The number of grafts to be transplanted in the sparse area is evaluated and other consultations are done. Then the day is determined for the hair transplantation surgery. If everything is appropriate, the surgery is usually started on the next day after this meeting. If necessary, the surgeon may request a mini check-up before the operation.
2
Evaluation of Medical History
Before the surgery, the health history of the patient is evaluated and the measures to be taken are determined. The questions that will be addressed to you will include smoking and alcohol use, chronic diseases, allergic condition, regularly used medications and whether you have had an operation before.
3
Operation
If everything is deemed appropriate, the hair transplantation surgery will start. The process will take approximately 5 to 6 hours, depending on the number of grafts to be transplanted. Hair transplantation can be done with the DHI or FUE method. Although they are similar in general, there are some differences but this does not significantly affect the duration of the surgery.
4
Discharge from Hospital
After the surgery, your donor area is closed with a bandage so that it does not get air, the area of transplantation is left open. You will be discharged after the procedure without having to stay in the hospital.
5
Aftercare
On the first day after the surgery, you will come back to our clinic for aftercare service. At this stage, your bandage is removed by your surgeon. If necessary, he/she will perform the washing process on the same or next day himself/herself.
6
Last Instructions and Return to Home Country
Once the aftercare stage is completed, your hair transplantation surgery is completed successfully. In the next process, you don’t need to come back to the clinic but you should follow the instructions of your doctor and wait for the process to become perfect.
