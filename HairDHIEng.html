Direct Hair Implantation (DHI)

Newer techniques are being developed to meet the expectations of people in hair transplantation. DHI (Direct Hair Implantation) is the newest of these techniques. DHI, the most advanced technique currently applied, meets the expectations of the people at the highest level.

What is DHI?

DHI, which is the most advanced technique used in hair transplantation, is a method that allows the hair follicles to be transplanted directly to the bald area as it can be understood from its name. The success rate is extremely high. In other methods, the channels are opened after the grafts are collected. In the DHI method, it is possible to transplant directly without opening the channel. For this, special items are used. The reduction of the time that the grafts are left out increases the success. The most important difference of the DHI technique is the pens known as the implanter or choi needle. The collected grafts are placed in these pens and transplantation is done directly without opening the channel. This advantage ensures that there are no cuts, sutures, scars and pain. Thus, compared to other methods, recovery period of this process shortens seriously.

Who Is Eligible for DHI?

DHI hair transplantation is a method that can be applied to almost anyone who does not have a very important health problem. The most suitable candidates for the DHI method are:
  • Those who have hair loss
  • People who are completely bald
  • Those who want to increase hair density
  • Those who want to have beard and mustache transplantation
  • Those who want to have eyebrow transplantation
  • Those who do not want to shave

Features and Benefits of DHI Hair Transplantation

The DHI method has many advantages compared to existing hair transplantation methods. The main ones are:
  • Recovery period is very short in DHI method. Because the holes opened by choi pens used in the operation are very small, there is almost no bleeding.
  • No need to shave. It will be sufficient to shave a small amount only in the donor area where the roots will be collected.
  • DHI is a more accurate choice for those who want to increase hair density. In the FUE method, an average of 60 grafts can be planted per cm2, while in the DHI method, up to 90 grafts can be transplanted.
  • In DHI method, up to 5500 grafts can be transplanted in a single session.
  • The risk of damaging the collected hair follicles is at minimum level. Because the collected graft is transplanted directly into the region without waiting.
  • There is no need to open a channel in DHI method. Grooving and planting is done simultaneously thanks to choi pens.

DHI Consultation

You will need to have a consultation with your doctor before starting DHI hair transplantation. The information that your doctor will ask you in this consultation is as follows:
  • Your current health status
  • Surgeries you have had in the past
  • All medicines you use (including herbal medicines)
  • Your allergy situation
  • Alcohol and smoking
In the light of all this information, your eligibility for DHI hair transplantation surgery will be determined. At this stage, you should discuss all your expectations from the operation with your doctor.

How is DHI carried out?

Some steps are followed in the application of the DHI technique. These steps and their explanations are given below.
1
Anesthesia
Local anesthesia is applied to the relevant area, so that the patient does not feel any pain both during the collection of grafts and transplantation.
2
Collection of Grafts
Hair is collected one by one after anesthesia. The technique used to collect the hair is the same as the method used in the FUE technique. At this step, it can be said that there is no difference compared to the DHI method.
3
Preparing the Grafts
The collected grafts are prepared for transplanting by technicians. At this step, tissues on the grafts are removed and care is taken to ensure that there is no foreign substance in the grafts.
4
Transplanting the Grafts
The most important difference in the DHI technique occurs at this step. First of all, grafts are placed in special items used in this technique. Then it is transferred to the area to be transplanted with a pencil. Thus, the pencil both opens the channel required for the graft and leaves the graft in this channel. Thus, two operations are done at the same time.

Risks and Complications of DHI Hair Transplantation

DHI risks are quite a few –almost none. Preventing complications that may occur during its operation depends entirely on the professionalism of the team. These risks are:
  • Bleeding
  • Ache
  • Swelling
  • Infection

Preparation for DHI Operation

Operations should be started at least 1 week before DHI hair transplantation is taken.
  • Alcohol and cigarette smoking affect the operation, thus you should stop smoking and taking alcohol at least 4 days in advance. Leaving it 7 days in advance helps you have a better operation.
  • Stop consuming caffeine-containing foods and drinks 3 days in advance.
  • Wash your hair and come to the operation with a clean scalp.
  • Never use hair gel, spray and similar hair care products in this process.
  • On the day of the operation, it is a good idea to bring loose or zippered clothes. It is important to choose this type of clothing in order not to touch your scalp while wearing it.
  • The use of aspirin and similar blood thinners should be discontinued at least 1 week in advance.

Care After DHI

There are also issues to be considered after DHI hair transplantation surgery. It is very important to follow your doctor's instructions on this matter. Things to consider about care after DHI:
  • Removing the bandage and washing the hair for the first time should definitely be done by your doctor in the clinic (after 24 to 36 hours).
  • You should wash your hair yourself every day after the first hair wash is done by your doctor. Take care to wash your hair gently every day for 15 days, as recommended by your doctor.
  • Use a soft and clean towel to dry your hair. Dry your hair with soft touches, never rub the towel on your hair!
  • It is not recommended to use a hair dryer. However, when you need it very much, it is okay to use it without turning on the hot mode and getting too close to your head.
  • After DHI hair transplantation surgery, it is recommended that you do not swim for 3-4 weeks and protect your head from the sun for the grafts to hold well.
  • Take great care to protect your head from blows for the first 15 days.
  • It is recommended to sleep at an angle of 15 degrees for the first 3 days to prevent temporary swelling with an anesthetic effect.
