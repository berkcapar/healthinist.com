HAIR TRANSFER FROM BODY

Hair affects the appearance of people significantly. Loss of hair as a result of falling causes the appearance to change and deteriorate. The most influential method that can be preferred in such a situation is hair transplantation. For hair transplantation, generally, hair follicles from the neck area are collected and replanted to the area with no hair. Ok, but what to do if there arenâ€™t enough grafts in the neck area? Can hair transplantation be performed with hair follicles collected from different parts of the body?

Can Hair Transplantation Be Performed with the Hair from the Body?

The main source of grafts for hair transplantation is the neck area. The reason is that these grafts are extremely resistant to falling. Therefore, transplantation performed using the grafts collected from the neck area provides natural and permanent hair. But, in some people, there may not be sufficient grafts in the neck area. It may be thought that hair transplantation is not possible in such cases. However, even in such cases, hair transplantation is possible with the hair follicles collected from different parts of the body. The first part to be preferred other than the neck area is the beard and chest part. The beard right below the chin can be used for hair transplantation. Again, if there are hair follicles on the chest, they can be used as well. If there are not enough grafts in these areas, different parts are examined. In short, hair follicles in any part of the body can be used for this procedure. But, the hair follicles must be strong enough.

How is Hair Transferred from the Body?

The techniques used in normal hair transplantation are similarly used here. First of all, grafts are collected one by one, canals are opened. After these stages, the transfer of grafts starts. Not all of the hair follicles collected from the body are directly transferred to the hairless area. The reason is that their structures, growth pattern and other features are different. Therefore, grafts collected from the neck area are used together with the grafts taken from the body. Hair follicles collected from the body are used to increase hair density. This means it is not possible to use them alone. They can be used together with the grafts collected from the neck area to increase the density. There are different techniques to be used for this procedure such as DHI and FUE. After this stage, the following steps are taken depending on the technique preferred. Hair transplantation performed is no different from normal hair transplantation and again a natural hair appearance is obtained.
